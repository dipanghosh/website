Rome: The Eternal City

A Trip to Rome: 24th -29th April 2017

Rome is absolutely in everyone's vacation list. So, I started my trip around Europe with Rome. I took leave from work for three days, combined with a holiday in between and weekends, it spanned over roughly a week (6 days). I started from Munich, flown into Da-Vinci Airport, and stayed near the Roma Terminus station. First day was vatican, next day was pretty much all of Rome. Thenext day was some rest and some more of Rome. For the next two days I went to Florence. Finally, the last day, before catching the flight at night, I went to see the St. Peters Bascillica by itself. Here are the photos of this trip, there is also a video in production, as a result of me documenting the trip. So, here we go.

Day 1 : Vatican

The first day 24th of Arpil I spent mostly in the vatican museums and wandering around outside the vatican walls. This day, I got the best picture in Rome also!

Day 2 : Rome

I met two people in the hostel I was staying at, day 2 was packed with going all around Rome with them. It was so jammed in fac, that the next day I had to take a break!

Day 3 : Take a break

Day 3 was a rest day. Did not sleep all last night, so time to get some sleep. Before that though, I visited a few places way up in the morning when there are no tourists!

Day 4 : Off to Florence

At this point Rome was pretty much covered. So I headed to Florence.

Day 5 : The Uffizi and Florence

This day I visited mostly the Uffizi gallery, which is a world renowned art museum. Had plans to go on top of the Duomo, but that did not end up happening.

Day 6 : St Peter's and back

It was time to head home, but before that during the day, I went inside the St. Peters and also climbed to the top!
