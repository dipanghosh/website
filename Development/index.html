Hello!

I am Dipan

Welcome to my little corner in the web. I am a researcher, photography enthusiast, and graphic designer. I am currently pursuing my PhD, and I sometime delve into photographic explorations and art. This is the platform where I hope to present and publish all that I do, because posting in social media is just too old! So feel free to look around and enjoy. If you have anything to say, please let me know, I would love to hear it!

Photographic Explorations

Photography is a combination of art and craft, it is as much technical as it is an art form. It is this marriage of art and technology in photography that has fascinated me. Having a strong scientific background, I am always keen to learn the techlogical details, and just blown away by the art that photography is. So, please have a look around, and explore my photographic vision!

Graphic Design

I have very limited expertise in traditional drawing, but that does not stop me from being artistic! Time to time I delve to create what my imaginations comes up with, and I love creative vector graphics. The boundaries of my design expertise is not very well defined, and I welcome you to check out my collection. You will find vector graphics, and also, molecules and proteins rendered in exquisite details!
