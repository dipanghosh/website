Swans of the Isar

Yesterday, 16th of March, I arrived super early to work, because I got up very early. That gave me the opportunity to leave early and go have a walk and shoot on the banks of the main river here in munich, Isar. The place was suggested to me, the weather was clear, so I thought I would take the chance.

I arrived at Thalkirchen timely, a little before the light was getting nice and golden. I started taking a few pictures here and there, tried taking nice potraits of the swans that were all around, it was great fun. Then, just when the light was at its best, an elderly couple pulled in a cartful of bird feed and a feeding frenzy ensued! This just made it all the more interesting I had a great lot of fun shooting. I shot through the golden hour on to the blue hour and a little after sunset, I headed back home

Have a look. This was my first attempt to shoot wildlife, and I learnt a great deal! Hope you enjoy!
