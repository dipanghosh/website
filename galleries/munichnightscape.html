Nightscapes: Munich

As the sun sets, our cities emerge in a new form. In the incandescent light, under the moon and the stars, the view of our city are transformed.

Recently, I bought my tripod, so I took it out to the highest point in Munich, the Olympiaturm, or Olympic tower, which was built around 1972 for the olympic games. From here, the entire city of munich is visible, and the view is breathtaking.

Enjoy! and if you have any comments, please let me know.
