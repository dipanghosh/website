Mittenwald: Meeting the Mighty Alps!

Alps is very close to Munich. Get a bavarian pass, catch a train, and your will find yourself surrounded by the Giants. The beauty of the immense landscape is quite intense, and the feeling is inexplicable. A camera can merely catch the lights that strikes its sensor, but that could never express the mighty beauty that the Alps is.

Mittenwald is a small town located right at the edge of German-Austrian border, in the midst of the  Alps. There is a cable car that goes on top of the montain, and the view is just gorgeous. This Sunday, 9th of April, I went to Mittenwald. The weather was fantastic, and I realized its true what they say. Alps, indeed is Mighty!

Have a look

There are beautiful birds and nature to shoot here, but the place is a little crowded. I will be back, and there will be better photos!
